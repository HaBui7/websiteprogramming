Treasure Island

Summary:This well-known adventure tale is well-known, but what is Treasure Island really about? The book Treasure Island was written by Robert Louis Stevenson in 1883. It played a significant role in the years that followed in the proliferation of media about pirates. A young boy named Jim Hawkins embarks on a perilous adventure to find buried treasure in the story. He runs into mutinous pirates, castaways who become stranded, terrifying ghost stories, and much more along the way. Since it was first released, audiences have been enthralled by Treasure Island, a coming-of-age tale about adventure, friendship, and redemption.
